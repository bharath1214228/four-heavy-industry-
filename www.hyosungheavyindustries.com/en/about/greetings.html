Greetings

Greetings

Thank you for visiting our homepage.

We at Hyosung Heavy Industries have accumulated many years of experience in heavy electric equipment, housing, architectural and civil engineering, etc. which make up the core of industrial sectors. We work in earnest to meet the demands of our markets based on sheer business competencies and unique technological know-how.

In heavy electric equipment, we tirelessly pursue innovation for competitive advantages in new growth industries like renewable energy with agility in line with the importance global customers place on the environment by establishing future-oriented power supply network systems such as ESS, STATCOM, etc. at the cusp of the Fourth Industrial Revolution.

In the construction sector, we are forging ahead with housing and architectural business activities based on our brand value based on integrity and convenience, while securing more stable growth engines by expanding into new sectors.

We are committed in earnest to engaging in customer-oriented management and providing differentiated brand value as a leading business. We value your sincere interest and generous support. Thank you.

Yang Dong-gi

CEO

Hyosung Heavy Industries
