Hyosung Way

A value system that leads mankind to live a better life

Hyosung Way

Our Value

  • The Hyosung Way is the value structure where all the Hyosung members around the world empower each other to make their dreams come true.
  • By outing the Hyosung Way into practice, Hyosung will become a global leader that enhances the quality of life of each customer.

MISSION

Enhance and enrich the quality of life for humanity with its leading technology and management capability. Hyosung exists so that the 'best people' can develop the 'leading technology' and the 'management capability' by offering products and services that maximize value for customers, and thus enhance the value of life for humanity.

THE CORE VALUES & PRINCIPLES

  • Global Excellence
    • Maximize the competitiveness with ceaseless self-improvement efforts to win in any situation
    • Blaze new trails in markets around the world with a global outlook
  • Innovation
    • Eliminate all inefficiencies that do not add value
    • Challenge new possibilities with a positive mindset
  • Accountability
    • Act like owners and take charge in actions
    • Never give up until achieving the goal
  • Integrity
    • Uphold transparency and fairness based on facts and principles
    • Respect and cooperate with each other to make a great workplace
