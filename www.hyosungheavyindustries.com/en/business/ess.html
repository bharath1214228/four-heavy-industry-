Energy Storage System (ESS)

Based on power conversion and energy operation technology, Hyosung Heavy Industries is leading the technology and market development of domestic energy storage systems. Hyosung Heavy Industries undertakes various activities in the Energy Storage System business ranging from supplying systems to maintenance and consulting business for construction. Based on our abundant business experiences and product reliability, Hyosung has strengths in the ESS business and contributes to maximizing customer value and creating new future value for the electric power industry.

Features and Benefits

Turn-Key Solution
Hyosung Heavy Industries, as a system supplier, provides a full ESS system (PCS, battery, PMS) to our customers.
ESS Experts and Maintenance System
We have the ability to solve potential problems that may arise during projects.
We place the right experts for each part at the forefront to realize customer satisfaction.
We increase reliability through system stabilization and quick response to system failures.
ESS Engineering and Designing Competency
With our experience as the being the leading power equipment company in Korea, we have a deep understanding of the grid system
Based on a large number of business experiences, we can design various applications and suggest the most optimal ESS specifications and operation plans through customer needs analysis.

Product Type

ESS can be applied to frequency regulation, peak shaving, and renewable integration. Renewable integration is often used for micro-grid in lieu of a conventional generator (diesel generator) in individual islands and deserts.

  • Peak Shaving
  • Frequency Regulator
  • Renewable Integration
  • Micro-grid

Peak Shaving

HVDC(High Voltage Direct Current transmission)
Uses

Ensure profitability by charging electricity when the cost (rate) is low and discharging it when cost is high

Shifting of maximum electricity demand time (Peak shifting)
Discharging Duration Over 2 Hours
Benefits

Consumer: reduces electricity costs

System operator: reduces risk of power shortage during peak time and reduces the investment costs for building more power plants.
Key Customers End-users
Installations

In 2012, as a domestic supply business, a 250kW/500kWh ESS was installed in Guri Agro-Fishery Market

A 6MW/32MWh ESS was installed at the Busan plant of Dongkuk Steel Mill Co., Ltd.

Frequency Regulator

HVDC(High Voltage Direct Current transmission)
Uses

Gird Assistance System: Provide emergency reserve

Power Quality Improvement: Adjust frequency of generated power

Discharging Duration 15~30 Mins. (Frequency Regulation)
Benefits

Electricity company: improves efficiency of existing generators

System operator: operates the grid system operation in a more stable manner thanks to quicker response and improves power quality
Key Customers Power Generator
Installations

4MW ESS to provide reserve power of Jeju's Jeochun substation and management of active / reactive power

48MW ESS for frequency regulation at Gimje substation

Renewable Integration

HVDC(High Voltage Direct Current transmission)
Uses

Electric energy supply at peak electricity demand time (REC profit obtained from weight differences)

Variation control of wind power and solar power output

Postponed investment for power system transmission capacity expansion
Discharging Duration 3~4 Hours
Benefits

Renewable power generation company: achieves additional gains by making use of REC weight differences after storage

System operator: resolves the instability of the power grid and saves the cost of operating the whole power grid by controlling waste or shortages of electricity
Key Customers Renewable Power Generation Company
Installations

Namdong Power (Youngheung Solar) 7MW / 18MWh ESS

Haenam Solar Connection 12.5MW/32MWh ESS

Pyeongchang Wind Power Connection

6MW/18MWh ESS

Micro-grid

Micro-grid
Uses

Variation control of wind power and solar power output

Postponed investment for power system transmission capacity expansion

Supply power to the grid-insufficient area through renewable integration
Discharging Duration 2~5 Hours
Benefits

Efficient sales of electricity generated through monitoring of sales price after storage

Saving cost of grid operation with controlling renewables power output

Installations
