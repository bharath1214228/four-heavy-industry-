ARC Welding Machine

Hyosung Heavy Industries is a comprehensive welding machine maker and has continuously endeavored to develop the welding machine industry in Korea. We are devoted to producing better products to realize the quality and performance that our customers require. Based on the power electronic control technology and digital communication fusion technology we have accumulated since our establishment, along with a great deal of know-how and experience in welding, we have developed Full Digital ARC Welding Machine with our own technology and have enabled high-quality welding for automobiles.

Features and Benefits

Full Digital Welding Control Technology
Hyosung Heavy Industries is the first in Korea to develop an Inverter ARC welding machine in 1992.
With the high speed neck control technology for low spatter welding, we are leading the automobile market by realizing welding quality for robots.
Robust Technology
With verified reliability that is confirmed by product design and accelerated lifetime test comparable to international standards, it contributes to the improvement of customer utilization rate by not causing malfunctions even under various welding environments.
Tailored Quality Design Technology (Sophisticated Technology)
To meet changes such as reduced automobile body weight and improved corrosion resistance and safety, new products are developed to satisfy customer specifications. Our designs are optimized for general welding as well as welding for robots.
Global Robot Line-Up
Interface including communication suitable for global robot makers such as ABB, Fanuc, YASKAWA, OTC, KAWASAKI, KUKA, has been developed and lined up to meet customer needs.
